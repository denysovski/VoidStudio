ORIGIN

Founded in 2019 from the underground Berlin scene. Every piece carries the DNA of industrial decay and post-human aesthetics.

Born from the ashes of conventional fashion. A rejection of color, form, and expectation.

PHILOSOPHY

Anti-fashion as resistance. We reject seasonal trends, embracing instead the eternal cycle of creation and destruction.

We design for the collapse. Garments that exist between moments, between identities.

MATERIAL

Salvaged military surplus, recycled synthetics, and organic cotton processed through experimental treatments.

Industrial fabrics transformed. Each thread carries the weight of forgotten futures.

PURPOSE

Protection through anonymity. Our garments shield the wearer from the chaos of modern existence.

To clothe the void within. Armor for navigating a world that no longer makes sense.
